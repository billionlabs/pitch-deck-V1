Our Special Sauce: Vibrometric Force Estimation

The Physics Behind the Magic

Step 1: Phone's vibration motor creates controlled oscillations
Step 2: Finger pressure dampens vibration amplitude
Step 3: Motion sensors detect vibration changes
Step 4: Algorithm converts to precise force measurement
Step 5: Combined with optical pulse data = Blood Pressure
